The Fox and the Wolves

The rules are simple. You'll need these items:

1. Flour, chalk powder, or anything else to mark places.

2. Some friends.

Someone in your friend group has to be the fox, and the rest will be wolves. You are the one Luna chose, so you will be the fox.

The fox gets a head start of 1 minute. Run as far as you can, marking your route with powder or chalk. Wolves will chase you by following the trail.

IMPORTANT: No cheating. Sprinkle powder every few meters. Team up with 1 wolf, who is your companion chosen by Luna. Breaking these rules will have consequences. ψ
